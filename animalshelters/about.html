About Us

Here at Bunnies and Bracelets, we help donors get their donations to local shelters here in Middlesex County, MA. Our website has links and donation pages for reliable animal shelters and their sites.

We display the shelters so that they are easy to find and people can access their sites easily.

These shelters are hand-picked and we carefully check their sites to make sure they are appropriate for our cause.
